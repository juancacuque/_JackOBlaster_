JackOBlaster.
Survivor.io-style game made as a class project in Unity. Pick a character, survive as long as you can, and build your loadout as you level up.
Controls.
WASD — move.
Left click — shoot.
How it works
At the start you pick your character from a selection screen. Wait for the timer to finish before clicking — selecting too fast causes a bug.
As you kill enemies you gain XP and level up. Each level up gives you 3 random upgrades to choose from. Pick one, keep going. Upgrades have a max level so there's a ceiling to how far you can push each one.
Upgrades include:
Rotating blades around your character
Damage aura.
Drones that shoot at enemies.
Mines.
Enemies:
Weak melee — fast and fragile, come straight at you.
Strong melee — slower but tankier.
Ranged — keep their distance and shoot at you.
